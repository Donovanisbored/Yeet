All About Jelly beans (and more!)

Jelly beans take six to ten days to make. There are two varities--traditional beans and gourmet. The gourmet beans are typically softer and smaller. Gourmet, unlike traditional, have flavor in both the center and the shell.

Not everyone likes jelly beans. You have to have a certain taste for it. Some quite enjoy these special candies!

For the most part, a jelly bean contains:
  • Sugar
  • Corn Syrup
  • Food Starch
The steps for jelly bean making include:
  1. Cooking and chemistry
  2. Starch casting
  3. Panning
  4. Sugar coating
  5. Packaging
