Wizard of Oz

  • In this film, Dorothy and her dog Toto, are caught in a tornado's path and somehow end up in the Land of Oz. Here she meets some memorable friends and foes. In her journey to meet the Wizard of Oz who everyone says can help her return home and possibly grant her new friends their desires (a brain, heart and courage).     • careworn ‐ showing the effect of grief or anxiety
      • caliginous ‐ black, misty, dark
